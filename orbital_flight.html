Orbital Flight

Description

Orbital flight represents a remarkable achievement in space exploration, as it involves the precise balancing of gravitational forces and velocity to enable an object to continuously circle a celestial body without falling back to the surface.

Key Features

  1. You can see the magnificent earth from outer space.
  2. You can feel microgravity for a good amount of time.
  3. Orbital tourism provides a once-in-a-lifetime adventure for tourists who dream of experiencing space travel
